Features

Play with unlimited number of friends

With Just Dance Now, there is no limit to the number of players that can join in and play Just Dance at the same time. Everyone in the room (or stadium, or classroom, or backyard, etc.) can play the game together at the same time, at any time. Players simply open the Just Dance Now app on their respective smartphones and enter the room number to join in the dance session.

Enjoy a social and personalized experience

Access your personal stats at any time and connect to Facebook to check out your friends’ achievements!

Video recording and sharing

Need a dance break? As part of the Just Dance Now app, you can record your friends and immortalize the moment when you were all having a blast – better than a boring old selfie! Replay the video at the end of the dance session and even share it on Facebook to keep the memory alive.

Access an ever-growing catalog of Just Dance greatest hits

A mix of hot tracks and classic gems that make the Just Dance tracklist so unique, and ensure memorable moments of fun!

How it works

Getting set up and playing Just Dance Now is simple and uses devices that many people already own.

  1. Launch

    Simply launch the Just Dance app on your Android device, iPhone, or iPod Touch.

  2. Connect

    Go to www.justdancenow.com on your computer, press Play and follow the on-screen instructions to link your app.

  3. Dance

    Then, hold your device in your right hand and follow the dancers on screen!
